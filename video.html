Scientific Timeline Breakdown

  • 0-10s: The Ecological Challenge — Synthetic fertilizers consume 2% of global energy and run off into waterways.
  • 10-20s: The Chemical Dialog — Soil *Rhizobia* release Nod factors, engaging receptor kinases (LysM-RLKs) on root hairs.
  • 20-30s: The Phosphate Switch — Jawahar's research shows how the PHR-L7 transcription factor acts as a molecular gateway.
  • 30-40s: Rewiring via CRISPR — Fine-tuning transcriptional circuits in cowpea and soybean for high field yields.
